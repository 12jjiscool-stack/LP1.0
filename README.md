### A raspberry pi is an os system and multi core chip that you can use for projects!

## How does it work? 
### The LP1.0 or formally Leaf phone version 1.0 is a small smart phone that is a mixture of an os and self phone. 
### i cost around 37$ to make on aliExspress with Savings (use honey) And that includes the 3.5 inch rp compatible lcd touch screen,
### the raspberry pi 3 itself and a OV5647 Camera module (1080p)

## Am i making an Os? 
### Yes i am making an os, me and my brother have been looking foward to this for awhile and are happy to get started finally after planning for months
